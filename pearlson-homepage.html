BRAND SPOTLIGHT — 02

Walling systems: AAC & Porotherm

Two structural walling systems, one goal — lighter buildings, faster timelines, and better thermal comfort than a conventional brick wall.

AAC BLOCKS — ⅓ THE DENSITY OF CLAY BRICK
Birla Aerocon · AAC Blocks

Strong, lightweight, and engineered from fly ash

Autoclaved Aerated Concrete blocks at roughly a third the density of a clay brick — laid faster, transported with less breakage, and made from recycled thermal-plant fly ash rather than quarried clay.

High fire resistance and strong thermal & sound insulation in every block.
Eco-friendly manufacturing — non-toxic, low-emission, built from recycled fly ash.
Faster site work — paired with Block Jointing Mortar, joints shrink from 10mm to 3mm with no water curing.

POROTHERM — 60% LIGHTER, 45% BETTER INSULATED
Wienerberger · Porotherm Smart Bricks

Clay engineering that breathes with the climate

Porotherm's honeycombed clay structure is 60% lighter than conventional brick and insulates 45% better — natural, fire-rated, and sourced from de-silted tank clay rather than fresh topsoil.

Naturally cooler interiors from superior thermal mass and insulation.
Fire-rated to F240 — 240 minutes of fire protection, fired at 1000°C.
Low water absorption — minimal risk of dampness, cracking or shrinkage.

OUR STORY

From rosewood furniture
to reinforced walls.

Pearlson & Co. is a family-owned business built in Chennai in 1915 — starting out manufacturing premium rosewood and teak furniture, long before it became one of South India's trusted names in building materials.

That same attention to material and craft carried into construction: today we're the leading Chennai dealer for Fenesta, Sumai, Birla Aerocon and Porotherm, working with architects, contractors and homeowners across Chennai and Pondicherry.

Four generations on, our name still stands for one thing — materials chosen to last, and the service to back them.
